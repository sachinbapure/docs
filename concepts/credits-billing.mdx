MailValid uses a credit-based pricing model: every fresh, completed verification costs 1 credit. Credits are deducted at the time of verification and — crucially — **they never expire**. Whether you're on a monthly plan or topping up pay-as-you-go, any credits you acquire stay in your account until you use them, with no time pressure.

## What uses a credit

Not every API call costs a credit. MailValid only charges you when it returns a useful, actionable result.

| Result | Credits used |
|---|---|
| Fresh `valid`, `invalid`, `catch_all`, or `do_not_mail` result | **1 credit** |
| `unknown` result (provider blocked, timeout) | **0 credits** |
| `cached: true` — recently verified same address | **0 credits** |
| Duplicate email in the same bulk upload | **0 credits** |

<Note>
  MailValid only bills you for useful results. If the API can't determine whether an address is deliverable — because a provider blocked the SMTP check, for example — you are not charged. The same applies to repeated checks on an address you've already verified recently.
</Note>

## Plans

Choose a monthly plan that matches your verification volume. All plans include access to both single and bulk verification endpoints.

| Plan | Monthly credits | Price | Rate limit | Max bulk size |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Free | 100 (one-time) | $0 | 10 req/min | 100 emails |
| Starter | 10,000/month | $15/month | 50 req/min | 2,000 emails |
| Growth | 50,000/month | $50/month | 100 req/min | 5,000 emails |
| Business | 200,000/month | $150/month | 200 req/min | 10,000 emails |

The Free plan includes 100 credits as a one-time allowance — no credit card required, and no monthly renewal. Paid plans renew monthly and include webhook notifications and bulk CSV/JSON export. The Growth plan is the most popular choice for teams sending at scale.

## Pay-as-you-go top-up

If your monthly plan credits run low — or you need a one-off burst of capacity — you can top up with pay-as-you-go credits. Top-ups start from **1,500 credits for $5** (300 credits per extra dollar). Top-up credits are billed once and **never expire**, so there's no risk of buying credits you won't use in time. You can combine a top-up with any active monthly plan.

## Credits never expire

Unused credits from any source — monthly plans or pay-as-you-go top-ups — **roll over indefinitely**. There is no end-of-month reset on unused credits, and there are no expiry dates. Buy credits when you have a campaign coming up, use them at your own pace, and know that nothing is wasted if you end up not needing them all.

This applies to monthly plan credits too: if you verify 8,000 out of your 10,000 Starter credits in a given month, the remaining 2,000 carry forward into the next billing period.

## Rate limits

Every plan has a per-minute rate limit on API requests. When you exceed your limit, the API returns a `429 Too Many Requests` response. Use the following response headers to track your usage and handle rate limiting gracefully in your integration:

| Header | Description |
|---|---|
| `X-RateLimit-Limit` | Maximum requests allowed in the current window |
| `X-RateLimit-Remaining` | Remaining requests in the current window |
| `Retry-After` | Seconds until the rate limit resets (included with 429 responses) |

When you receive a `429`, read the `Retry-After` value and pause your requests for that duration before retrying. For high-volume use cases, the Business plan's 200 req/min limit accommodates most production workloads. Contact support if you need a custom limit beyond that.

## Managing billing

You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time from the [dashboard](https://mailvalid.io/dashboard).

- **Upgrading** takes effect immediately — you gain access to the higher credit allowance and rate limits straight away.
- **Downgrading** takes effect at the end of your current billing period — you keep your existing plan benefits until then.
- **Cancelling** also takes effect at the end of the current billing period. Your existing credits are always kept, regardless of plan changes or cancellation.

There are no setup fees, no contracts, and no penalties for changing plans. Visit [mailvalid.io/pricing](https://mailvalid.io/pricing) for the full pricing page and a current comparison with other email verification services.
