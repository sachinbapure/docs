## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do credits expire?

No, credits never expire. Use them whenever you need.

### What counts as a verification?

A fresh completed result uses 1 credit. Invalid addresses are billable because they are completed checks that help you avoid bounces. Duplicate emails in the same bulk upload, recently verified repeats, and inconclusive checks are free.

### What's included in verification?

Syntax check, domain/MX verification, SMTP mailbox verification, disposable detection, role-based detection, and catch-all detection.

### Is there an API rate limit?

Yes — limits scale with your plan: **Free 10**, **Starter 50**, **Growth 100**, and **Business 200** requests per minute. Bulk jobs allow up to **100 / 2,000 / 5,000 / 10,000** emails per job (Free / Starter / Growth / Business). Contact us for higher limits.
